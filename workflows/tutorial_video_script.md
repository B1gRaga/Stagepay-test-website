# StagePay — Video Ad / Tutorial Script

**Format:** Screen-recorded product demo + voiceover  
**Duration:** ~90 seconds (30-second cut included)  
**Tone:** Confident, clean, no fluff  
**Target audience:** Freelancers, consultants, small business owners  
**Tools needed:** Screen recording software (Loom, OBS, or Windows Game Bar) + any free voiceover tool (ElevenLabs, Descript, or record yourself)

---

## Pre-Production Checklist

Before you record, set up the following demo state in StagePay:
- [ ] Log in as a demo account (or your real account)
- [ ] Have at least 3 existing invoices on the dashboard: one **Paid**, one **Overdue**, one **Sent**
- [ ] Have 2–3 clients saved with names and WhatsApp numbers
- [ ] Settings filled out: firm name, brand colour, bank details
- [ ] Browser zoomed to ~110%, dark mode on, full screen
- [ ] Have the public invoice link for one invoice ready to paste into the browser

## SCENE-BY-SCENE STORYBOARD

---

### SCENE 1 — The Hook [0:00 – 0:08]

**On screen:**  
Black screen. White text fades in one line at a time (do this in Canva or CapCut):
> *"You finished the job."*  
> *"Now comes the hard part."*

**Voiceover:**
> "You finished the job. You did the work. But the invoice is still sitting in your head — and the money isn't moving."

**Cut to:** StagePay dashboard loading. The **Overdue** stat card is visible in red.

---

### SCENE 2 — The Dashboard [0:08 – 0:16]

**On screen:**  
Slow pan into the dashboard. Hover over the three stat cards: **Unpaid**, **Overdue**, **Paid This Month**. Scroll to the recent invoices list — show the status pills (Sent, Overdue, Paid).

**Voiceover:**
> "StagePay is a professional invoicing platform built for people who'd rather be working than chasing payments. Everything you need, in one place."

---

### SCENE 3 — Create an Invoice with AI [0:16 – 0:38]

**On screen:**  
Click **New Invoice** in the sidebar (or press `N`). The split-screen editor appears.

Type into the AI description box (slowly so viewers can read):
> *"Brand identity project for Kgosi Holdings — logo, stationery, and brand guide. R45,000 total, 50% deposit required, 14-day terms, 15% VAT"*

Click **Generate**. Watch the right-side live preview populate in real time — client, line items, deposit amount, VAT, due date, total and balance due.

Pause on the completed preview for 2 seconds. Point the cursor at the deposit row.

**Voiceover:**
> "Describe the work in plain English. StagePay's AI builds the invoice instantly — line items, VAT, deposit amounts, due dates. The live preview updates as you type. Edit anything with a click."

---

### SCENE 4 — Send via Email or WhatsApp [0:38 – 0:52]

**On screen:**  
Click **Send Invoice**. The send modal appears — show both **Email** and **WhatsApp** options. Click **WhatsApp**, select a client from the contact picker, hit **Send**.

**Cut to:** Open the public invoice URL in a new browser tab. Show what the client sees — the beautifully formatted invoice with your firm branding, line items, bank details at the bottom. Scroll slowly.

**Voiceover:**
> "Send instantly via email or WhatsApp. Your client gets a link to a professional invoice — no login required, no attachments to download. They see it, you get paid."

---

### SCENE 5 — Mark as Paid + Confirmation [0:52 – 1:05]

**On screen:**  
Go to the **Invoices** page. Tick the checkbox on a "Sent" invoice. The bulk action bar appears at the bottom. Click **Mark as Paid**. A confirmation modal appears — toggle **Send payment received notification** to on. Hit **Confirm**.

Brief cut to: a phone mockup showing a WhatsApp message arriving: *"Payment received — thank you! Invoice #007 is now settled."*

**Voiceover:**
> "When payment lands, mark the invoice paid in one click — and StagePay sends your client an automatic payment confirmation. Professional, instant, zero effort."

---

### SCENE 6 — Auto-Reminders [1:05 – 1:18]

**On screen:**  
Click **Reminders** in the sidebar. Show the invoice list with urgency colour-coding (yellow → orange → red). Hover over the auto-reminder schedule (3 / 7 / 14 / 30 days). Toggle auto-reminders on for an overdue invoice.

**Voiceover:**
> "For invoices that need a nudge — set auto-reminders. StagePay follows up at 3, 7, 14, and 30 days automatically, via email or WhatsApp. You don't have to chase anyone."

---

### SCENE 7 — CTA [1:18 – 1:30]

**On screen:**  
Fade to dark. StagePay logo animates in. Then:

> **"Professional invoicing."**  
> **"Start free — no credit card."**  
> **stagepay.app**

**Voiceover:**
> "StagePay. Start free today."

**Optional:** Minimal background music throughout (Epidemic Sound or Pixabay — search "corporate minimal"). Fade out on CTA.

## Recording Tips

| Tip | Detail |
|-----|--------|
| Resolution | Record at 1920×1080 or higher |
| Mouse | Move slowly and deliberately — viewers need to track you |
| Zoom | Browser at 110–120% so UI is large and readable |
| Editing | Trim silences; speed up the AI generation moment slightly |
| Captions | Always add subtitles — 85% of social video is watched on mute |
| Length | Aim for exactly 90 seconds for LinkedIn, YouTube, Instagram |

## Suggested Tools (all have free tiers)

- **Screen recording:** [Loom](https://loom.com) (easiest), OBS Studio (most control), Windows Game Bar (Win+G)
- **Video editing:** CapCut (free, simple), DaVinci Resolve (free, powerful)
- **Voiceover:** ElevenLabs (AI voice), Descript (record + edit), or record yourself with a phone mic
- **Background music:** Pixabay Music (free, no attribution needed)
