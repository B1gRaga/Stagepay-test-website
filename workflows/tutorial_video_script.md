# StagePay — Video Ad / Tutorial Script

**Format:** Screen-recorded product demo + voiceover  
**Duration:** ~90 seconds  
**Tone:** Confident, clean, no fluff  
**Target audience:** Freelancers, consultants, small business owners  
**Tools needed:** Screen recording software (Loom, OBS, or Windows Game Bar) + any free voiceover tool (ElevenLabs, Descript, or record yourself)

---

## Pre-Production Checklist

Before you record, set up the following demo state in StagePay:
- [ ] Log in as a demo account (or your real account)
- [ ] Have at least 2–3 existing invoices visible on the dashboard (one overdue, one paid)
- [ ] Have 2–3 clients saved
- [ ] Settings filled out: firm name, logo, brand colour
- [ ] Browser zoomed to ~110%, dark mode on, full screen

---

## SCENE-BY-SCENE STORYBOARD

---

### SCENE 1 — The Hook [0:00 – 0:08]

**On screen:**  
Black screen with white text fading in (can do this in Canva or CapCut):
> *"You finished the job."*  
> *"Now comes the hard part."*

**Voiceover:**
> "You finished the job. You did the work. But the invoice is still sitting in your head — and money isn't moving."

**Cut to:** StagePay dashboard loading — unpaid and overdue cards visible.

---

### SCENE 2 — The Dashboard [0:08 – 0:18]

**On screen:**  
Slow pan / zoom into the dashboard. Hover over the stat cards (Unpaid, Overdue, Paid This Month). Scroll down to show recent invoices list.

**Voiceover:**
> "StagePay is a professional invoicing platform built for people who'd rather be working than chasing payments."

**Key visuals:** Green accent, clean dark UI, real numbers in the stat cards.

---

### SCENE 3 — Create an Invoice with AI [0:18 – 0:42]

**On screen:**  
Click **New Invoice** in the sidebar. The split-screen editor appears.

Type into the AI description box (slowly, so viewers can read):
> *"Website redesign for Kgosi Holdings — 3 milestones, R15,000 each, 14-day payment terms, 15% VAT"*

Click **Generate**. Watch the live preview on the right populate in real time — client name, line items, VAT calculation, due date, total.

Scroll through the preview panel briefly.

**Voiceover:**
> "Just describe the job in plain English. StagePay's AI reads your description and builds the invoice — line items, VAT, due dates, all of it — in seconds."

**Pause on the completed live preview for 2 seconds.**

---

### SCENE 4 — Send It [0:42 – 0:55]

**On screen:**  
Click **Send Invoice**. The send modal opens. Show two options: Email and WhatsApp. Click **WhatsApp**. A contact picker appears — select a client. Hit **Send**.

Brief cut to: a phone mockup showing the WhatsApp message arriving with the invoice link (can be a static image in CapCut/Canva).

**Voiceover:**
> "Send it instantly — by email or WhatsApp. No printing, no PDFs lost in inboxes. Your client gets a link, sees the invoice, and can pay."

---

### SCENE 5 — Auto-Reminders [0:55 – 1:10]

**On screen:**  
Click **Reminders** in the sidebar. Show the invoice list with urgency colour-coding. Hover over the auto-reminder schedule sidebar (3-day, 7-day, 14-day, 30-day tiers).

Toggle a reminder on for an overdue invoice. The toggle animates green.

**Voiceover:**
> "Set automatic payment reminders — StagePay follows up for you at 3, 7, 14, and 30 days. Politely at first. Firmly when it matters. You don't have to chase anyone."

---

### SCENE 6 — Clients & Settings [1:10 – 1:20]

**On screen:**  
Quick 2-second cut to the Clients page (grid of client cards).  
Quick 2-second cut to Settings — show brand colour, firm name, bank details filled in.

**Voiceover:**
> "Manage all your clients in one place. Customise your branding, currency, VAT rates, and bank details — so every invoice looks like it came from a real firm."

---

### SCENE 7 — CTA [1:20 – 1:30]

**On screen:**  
Fade to dark. StagePay logo animates in (bar chart icon + wordmark). Then tagline:

> **"Professional invoicing."**  
> **"Start free — no credit card."**  
> **stagepay.app**

**Voiceover:**
> "StagePay. Start free today."

**Optional:** Upbeat, minimal background music throughout (Epidemic Sound or Pixabay — search "corporate minimal" or "clean tech"). Fade music out on the CTA.

## Recording Tips

| Tip | Detail |
|-----|--------|
| Resolution | Record at 1920×1080 or higher |
| Mouse | Move slowly and deliberately — viewers need to track you |
| Zoom | Use browser zoom (110–120%) so UI elements are large and readable |
| Editing | Trim silences, add captions, speed up typing sections slightly |
| Captions | Add subtitles — 85% of social video is watched on mute |
| Length | Aim for exactly 90 seconds for social (LinkedIn, Instagram Reels, X) |

## Suggested Tools (all free tiers available)

- **Screen recording:** [Loom](https://loom.com) (easiest), OBS Studio (most control), Windows Game Bar (Win+G)
- **Video editing:** CapCut (free, simple), DaVinci Resolve (free, powerful)
- **Voiceover:** ElevenLabs (AI voice), Descript (record + edit), or just record yourself with a phone mic
- **Background music:** Pixabay Music (free, no attribution needed)
